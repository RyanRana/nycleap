# Community Leader Quick Start Guide

## For Community Leaders

### Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**
   - Go to ReforestNYC
   - Click anywhere to open auth modal
   - Select "Community Leader"
   - Enter email and password
   - Click "Create Account"

2. **Choose Your Organization**
   - Search for your organization
   - If it exists: Click to select
   - If it doesn't exist:
     - Click "Create New Organization"
     - Enter organization name
     - Add description (optional but recommended)
     - Add at least one link (website, Twitter, Instagram, or Facebook)
     - Click "Create Organization"
   - Click "Next: Select Zipcodes"

3. **Select Your Zipcodes**
   - Click on each zipcode where your organization is active
   - You can select multiple zipcodes
   - Selected zipcodes will show a checkmark
   - Click "Next: Complete Profile"

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your role (e.g., "Director", "Coordinator")
   - Write a short bio about yourself
   - Upload a profile photo (optional)
   - Click "Complete Setup"

### Posting Events and News

1. **Access Dashboard**
   - Click "Dashboard" button in top-left corner
   - You'll see your statistics and existing posts

2. **Create a Post**
   - Click "+ Create Event/Post"
   - Choose post type:
     - **Event**: Community event with date/time
     - **News**: News article or update
     - **Information**: Educational content
     - **Announcement**: General announcement
   - Enter title and description
   - For events: Add date, time, and location
   - Upload an image (optional, max 5MB)
   - Add external link (optional)
   - Select zipcodes to tag this post to
   - Click "Create Post"

3. **Manage Posts**
   - View all your posts in the dashboard
   - Click "Delete" to remove a post
   - Posts appear immediately in tagged zipcodes

### Tips for Success

- **Clear Titles**: Use descriptive titles (e.g., "Tree Planting Event - Prospect Park")
- **Detailed Descriptions**: Include all relevant information
- **Use Images**: Posts with images get more attention
- **Tag Appropriately**: Only tag zipcodes where the content is relevant
- **Keep Updated**: Delete outdated events
- **External Links**: Link to registration pages, more info, etc.

## For Regular Users

### Viewing Organizations

1. **On the Map**
   - Click any neighborhood on the map
   - Scroll down in the sidebar
   - Find "Active Organizations" section
   - Click "+" to expand organization details
   - See community leaders and their roles
   - Click social links to visit organization pages

### Viewing Events

1. **By Neighborhood**
   - Click any neighborhood on the map
   - Scroll down in the sidebar
   - Find "Community Events & News" section
   - Click "+" to expand event details
   - See event images, dates, locations
   - Click "Learn More" for external links

2. **On Landing Page**
   - Scroll down from the main search bar
   - See "Community Events & News" section
   - Browse all recent events across NYC
   - Click "+" to expand any event

## Common Questions

### For Community Leaders

**Q: Can I edit an event after posting?**
A: Currently, you need to delete and recreate the event. Editing functionality is planned for the future.

**Q: How many zipcodes can I select?**
A: You can select as many as your organization actively serves.

**Q: Can I be part of multiple organizations?**
A: Currently, each community leader account is tied to one organization. You would need separate accounts for multiple organizations.

**Q: How do I update my organization's information?**
A: Contact the person who created the organization, as only the creator can update it.

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP are supported. Maximum 5MB for event images, 2MB for profile images.

**Q: Can I schedule posts for the future?**
A: Not currently. All posts are published immediately.

### For Everyone

**Q: How do I find community leaders in my area?**
A: Click your neighborhood on the map and look for the "Active Organizations" section in the sidebar.

**Q: Can I contact community leaders directly?**
A: You can see their email addresses and reach out to them. A messaging system may be added in the future.

**Q: How often are events updated?**
A: Events appear immediately when posted and remain visible until deleted by the community leader.

**Q: Can I RSVP to events?**
A: Currently, you need to use the external link if provided. RSVP functionality may be added in the future.
